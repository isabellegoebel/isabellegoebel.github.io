AQMEL

I modeled indoor air quality in various buildings across the UC Berkeley campus using data from PurpleAir sensors. My analysis focused on comparing the indoor-outdoor air quality ratio and diurnal hourly averages between newer and older buildings. Additionally, I examined how wildfires impacted indoor air quality, highlighting significant differences based on building age and ventilation systems.

Balance Hydrologics

I worked on improving the flood early warning system at Belmont Creek at El Camino. The current system alerts when the water Stage Height exceeds 4 feet, indicating imminent flooding, but it doesn't provide much lead time. To address this, I focused on modeling the 1D time series data from the Belmont site. My goal was to find reliable time-dependent relationships that can enhance the predictive capabilities and provide earlier warnings.
